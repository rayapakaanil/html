Early Life: Pawan Kalyan was born on September 2, 1971, in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh, India. He hails from the illustrious Konidela family, which is known for its contributions to the Telugu film industry

Film Career: Pawan Kalyan made his acting debut in 1996 with the Telugu film "Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi." However, he gained immense popularity and a cult following with movies like "Tholi Prema," "Gabbar Singh," "Attarintiki Daredi," and "Jalsa." He is known for his unique style, charismatic screen presence, and action-packed roles

Political Career: In addition to his film career, Pawan Kalyan is actively involved in politics. He has contested in elections and has been a vocal advocate for various social and political issues
